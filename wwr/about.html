Our History

The Rafting Project was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who sought to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with others. Starting with a small fleet of rafts and a makeshift launch site, the company quickly grew in popularity as word of its exciting and safe adventures spread. Over the years, the Rafting Project has expanded its operations to include a variety of trip options, from gentle floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, and has become a leader in the industry, known for its experienced guides, top-notch equipment, and commitment to environmental sustainability. Today, the Rafting Project continues to inspire and thrill adventurers of all ages and skill levels, offering an unforgettable experience on the water.
